What We Offer: Making AI Predictions Transparent with LIME.

We provide you with clear, understandable health predictions using advanced AI tools like SHAP and LIME. These tools break down complex predictions into easy-to-follow insights:

  • SHAP shows how each factor—like blood pressure, cholesterol, or lifestyle—affects your health risk predictions, helping you see exactly what drives the results.
  • LIME explains individual predictions, focusing on your specific case to clarify why the model made a particular decision about your health.

With LIME, we make AI predictions transparent, building trust and empowering you to take actionable steps towards better health.
